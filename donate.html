OUR MISSION

Book Fairies sources and redistributes books to under-resourced communities on Long Island and
New York City to provide equitable opportunities for children to learn, grow, and thrive.

HOW YOU CAN HELP

Join a movement to build stronger communities through increased access to books and literacy-based resources. Together, we can create a culture of literacy and a love of reading in under-resourced areas across Long Island and New York City.

BOOK FAIRIES GETS SOCIAL

GIVE SYLVIE A CHANCE AT A GOOD EDUCATION
Books have the power to change lives and empower communities. Access to high interest books inspires kids to be lifelong readers and narrows the reading proficiency gaps between high and low-income neighborhoods. By flooding under-resourced areas with necessary books, Book Fairies is building stronger communities, making a positive impact on literacy, and expanding life opportunities for all book recipients.
